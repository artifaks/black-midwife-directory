Why Your Support Matters

Black women are 3 to 4 times more likely to die from pregnancy-related causes than white women. This crisis persists regardless of income or education level.

Black Birth Connect exists to close this gap by making it easy for families to find culturally competent midwives who understand their needs and advocate for their safety.

  • Maintain and grow our free midwife directory
  • Publish maternal health education resources
  • Verify and vet listed practitioners
  • Support the Black Agrarian Directory
  • Build tools for birth justice advocacy
  • Keep the platform ad-free and accessible
